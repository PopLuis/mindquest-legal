Collecting and Using Your Personal Data

Types of Data Collected

Personal Data

The Application does NOT require You to create an account, and we do NOT collect any personally identifiable information such as:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Physical address
  • Date of birth

Usage Data

Usage Data is collected automatically when using the Application. Usage Data may include information such as:

  • Your Device's Internet Protocol address (e.g. IP address) — collected by AdMob
  • Device type, operating system and version
  • Advertising identifier (AAID on Android, IDFA on iOS)
  • Time and date of Application usage
  • Application crash reports (if any)
  • Pages or screens viewed within the Application

Locally Stored Data

The Application stores the following data locally on Your Device only (not transmitted to us):

  • Game statistics (scores, games played, correct answers)
  • User settings (sound preferences, username used in-game)
  • A randomly generated anonymous device identifier for analytics

This local data can be cleared at any time by uninstalling the Application or by clearing app data in Your device settings.

Use of Your Personal Data

The Developer may use Usage Data for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain our Service, including monitoring usage.
  • To display advertisements through Google AdMob, including personalized advertising based on Your advertising identifier.
  • To improve the Application, to analyze trends and understand how the Service is used.
  • To detect, prevent and address technical issues and prevent fraud or abuse.

Sharing Your Personal Data

We may share Your data with:

  • Google AdMob (Google LLC, USA): For displaying advertisements. Google AdMob may collect and use data according to its own privacy policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

If You are a resident of the European Economic Area (EEA) or the United Kingdom, You have certain data protection rights. The Developer aims to take reasonable steps to allow You to correct, amend, delete, or limit the use of Your Personal Data.

You have the following rights:

  • The right to access, update or delete the information We have on You.
  • The right of rectification — to have Your information corrected if inaccurate.
  • The right to object to Our processing of Your Personal Data.
  • The right of restriction — to request that We restrict the processing of Your information.
  • The right to data portability — to receive a copy of Your data.

You can exercise these rights by contacting us at the email below. You also have the right to complain to a Data Protection Authority about Our collection and use of Your Personal Data.

Opting Out of Personalized Advertising

You can opt out of personalized advertising by:

  • On Android: Go to Settings → Google → Ads → Enable "Opt out of Ads Personalization"
  • On iOS: Go to Settings → Privacy → Apple Advertising → Disable "Personalized Ads"

If You are a parent or guardian and You are aware that Your child has provided Us with Personal Data, please contact Us. If We become aware that We have collected Personal Data from a child, We will take steps to remove that information.

You can disable notifications at any time from your device settings or from within the Application.
